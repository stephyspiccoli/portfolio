OnRecycle App Design

Overview

OnRecycle aims to provide easily accessible information by having a mobile calendar which notifies the user of their regularly scheduled collection date, as well as specific drop-off locations and times for specialty items that can be recycled such as batteries and electronics. The purpose is to increase recycling and reduce waste.

The logo consists of a house encompassing the universally recognized recycling symbol on a green background. This is mean to reflect the "green" intentions of this app. The house is meant to indicate that this is for home use.

The Process

The App

The first step is to add your address, so you can get an accurate calendar of weekly garabage collection for your location.

Your local weekly calendar will be downloaded and displayed in your app, where you can also sync to your personal calendar.

On the Recycle options the user is able to view how to go about different items such as batteries or yard waste.

The user is able to read more about that specific waste and find locations within your area that you are able to deposit these specific items.
