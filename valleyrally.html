Overview

Valley Rally is looking to develop a brand and a website. The adventure tours will be marketed to an international audience, but specifically with Canadians in mind. The goal is to represent Valley Rally as a leader in adventure sports, not just in Canada, but worldwide.

The objective was to create an easy to use website that conveys the free spirit of Valley Rally in its imagery of riders and environments. All key information will be presented in a clear method that is consistent with the developed brand. The brand will focus on the adventurous aspect of the experience.

Logo Concept

The font choice conveys strength, and symbolizes the sturdy foundations of a well constructed bridge. The strength of the font represents the quality of the company’s work when building and designing structures.

The colours used are three shades of blue chosen by the company and they are used in the logo to create the illusion of depth. The combination of the evenly spaced arches represent a simple and modern bridge and creates a pleasing geometric look. The three arch combination is also meant to represent the company’s three founding engineers.
