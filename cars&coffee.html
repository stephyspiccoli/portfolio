Overview

Cars & Coffee is an event for car aficionado based in Ottawa. The event happens every year at Landsdowne Park and is a great opportunity for friends and family to hang out, have a coffee, and admire beautiful luxury and exotic cars.

The outcome was to create a full brand behind this awesome event that included a logo, business cards, posters and pictures for promotional use and to include in posters.

Posters

All pictures for the poster were taken by me. Various poster were created for several different platforms such as Intagram, Facebook and printed posters.
