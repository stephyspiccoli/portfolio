Overview

Cars & Coffee is an event for the car aficionado based in Ottawa. The event happens every year at Lansdowne Park and is a great opportunity for friends and family to hang out, have a coffee, and admire beautiful luxury and exotic cars.

The outcome was to create a full brand behind this awesome event that included a logo, business cards, posters and pictures for promotional use.

The colours chosen are bright and represent the fun behind the event. The font Eurostile represents strength and the sleekness of the luxury and sports cars at the event.
